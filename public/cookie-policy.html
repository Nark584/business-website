Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Marketing Cookies: These are used to track visitors across websites for advertising purposes
  • Preference Cookies: These remember your choices and preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Essential
Session Cookies Maintain your session while browsing Until browser closes Yes
Analytics Cookies Track website usage and performance 2 years No
Marketing Cookies Deliver targeted advertisements 1 year No
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and choices 1 year No

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from services such as:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Google Tag Manager: To manage tracking codes and tags
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing and integration
  • Advertising Networks: To display relevant advertisements

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads Settings
  • Facebook Ad Preferences

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • Forms may not save your progress
  • Personalized content may not be available
  • Website performance may be affected
  • Some features may not work as expected

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Decline non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
