Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Japanese Life Guide uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our website's performance
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Through your browser settings
  • Using our cookie consent banner
  • By disabling cookies in your browser preferences

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on our website, including Google Analytics and advertising networks.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through our contact form.
